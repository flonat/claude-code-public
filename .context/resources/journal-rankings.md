# Journal Rankings Reference

> Global reference for targeting publications. When choosing a journal, aim for **CABS AJG 4 or 4*** wherever possible.

## Ranking Systems

| System | Source | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **WBS 60** | UK business school internal list | 60 journals; used for REF and hiring |
| **FT 50** | Financial Times Research Rank | 50 journals; global MBA/business school ranking input |
| **CABS AJG** | Chartered Association of Business Schools | 1-4* scale; most comprehensive UK list |

Full CABS AJG 2024 spreadsheet: `.context/resources/journal-rankings/CABS-AJG-2024.xlsx`

|---------|--------|-------|------|-------------|
| Management Science | Yes | Yes | 4* | Methods + applications |
| Operations Research | Yes | Yes | 4* | OR theory + algorithms |
| Journal of Operations Management | Yes | Yes | 4* | Applied operations / field experiments |
| Production and Operations Management | Yes | Yes | 4 | Behavioral operations |
| OBHDP | Yes | Yes | 4 | Decision-making + cognition |
| Organization Science | Yes | Yes | 4* | Organisational theory |
| Journal of Management Studies | Yes | Yes | 4 | Broad management |
| Nature Human Behaviour | Yes | No | — | High-impact behavioural science |
| Journal of Applied Psychology | Yes | Yes | 4 | Applied behavioural science |
| Journal of Experimental Psychology: General | Yes | No | 4 | Experimental decision-making |
